# Privacy Policy for Bug Ball Blitz

**Effective Date:** November 13, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 13, 2025

## Introduction

Bug Ball Blitz ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile game application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
Bug Ball Blitz stores game progress and settings locally on your device:
- Player profile names
- Game statistics (wins, losses, goals scored)
- Achievement progress
- Customization preferences
- Game settings

**Important:** All game data is stored locally on your device using browser/app storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information on external servers.

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

#### AdMob Advertising
Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- IP address and approximate location
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
- App usage data for ad personalization

**AdMob Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### Analytics (If Implemented)
Currently, Bug Ball Blitz does not use analytics services. If we implement analytics in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Payment or financial information
- Photos, camera, or microphone access
- Contacts or calendar data
- Location data (beyond what AdMob may collect for ad serving)

## How We Use Information

### Local Data Usage
Information stored locally on your device is used solely to:
- Save your game progress
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Track achievements and statistics
- Provide a personalized gaming experience

### Advertising
AdMob uses collected data to:
- Display relevant advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party Service Providers
We integrate the following third-party service:

**Google AdMob (Advertising)**
- Provider: Google LLC
- Purpose: Display advertisements in the App
- Data Collected: See "AdMob Advertising" section above
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (see "Your Choices" below)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
All game data is stored locally on your device using:
- Browser localStorage (Web version)
- App storage/SharedPreferences (Android version)
- App storage/UserDefaults (iOS version)

### Security Measures
We implement reasonable security measures to protect data stored locally. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

### Data Retention
Game data remains on your device until you:
- Uninstall the App
- Clear app data/cache
- Delete your profile within the App

## Your Choices and Rights

### Advertising Preferences
You can limit ad personalization:

**Android:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads
2. Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

**iOS:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking
2. Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
3. Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Apple Advertising
4. Enable "Limit Ad Tracking"

### Data Deletion
To delete your local game data:
- **In-App:** Delete your profile from the profile management screen
- **Android:** Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Bug Ball Blitz ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Data
- **iOS:** Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage ‚Üí Bug Ball Blitz ‚Üí Delete App
- **Web:** Clear browser cache/localStorage for the game's website

## Children's Privacy

Bug Ball Blitz is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our App:
- Does not require account registration
- Does not collect personal information
- Uses family-friendly ad settings when applicable

Parents can control in-app purchases and ad tracking through device parental controls.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

Since Bug Ball Blitz stores data locally and does not collect personal information, these rights primarily apply to AdMob's data practices. Contact Google for AdMob-related requests: https://support.google.com/policies/troubleshooter/7575787

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

Since data is stored locally, you have direct control. For AdMob data, contact Google at: https://support.google.com/policies/troubleshooter/7575787

AdMob complies with GDPR consent requirements. When required, you will be prompted to consent to personalized ads before they are displayed.
